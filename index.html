Mariam Condilious
Memorial High School

Mariam Condilious Memorial High School is a Co-educational Hindi Medium School was established in 1999. Affiliated to UP board. With student strength of 350-400 and 18 teachers. Focusing on primary to tenth standard , imparting quality education to students and encourages other childrens to join school and complete their minimal and compulsary education.

Principal's message

It is with great pride and excitement that I write this letter to you as the Principal of Mariam Condilious Memorial High School. I am excited and honored to have the opportunity to lead a school with such a rich tradition both in and out of the classroom.
Education is not just a process of giving knowledge for a future job but a lifelong process which creates an understanding of moral and ethical values to guide one’s life in a right path. As a parent, you want the best for your child and we are proud to be working with you to ensure that they are given the right knowledge to face the future challenges. Parents, students and the school authorities are a team and we have to complement each other’s efforts.
